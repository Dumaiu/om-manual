Uniform Resource Identifier, URI

Compact sequence of characters that identifies an abstract or physical resource. Such identification enables interaction with representations of the resource over a network (typically the World Wide Web) using specific protocols.

Examples
  • http://lisp.ystok.ru/yuri
  • ftp://ftp.ietf.org/rfc/rfc3986.txt
